Vision
Scope

● To provide coaching support towards the development of a positive mindset towards an excellent academic outcome for their learners.

● To provide coaching to change the mindset of typical teachers to the growth mindset

● To provide coaching support towards Leadership and problem-solving skills.

CoachFrika

CoachFrika is a business enterprise that provides coaching support for teachers both in public and private schools across Nigeria towards excellent education, At CoachFrika, we offer hybridized coaching support throughout the academic session for our teachers. Our coaching system focuses on developing the professional capacity of the teacher at an early stage in the teaching profession towards the right mindset for improved learning outcomes, leadership skills, and problem-solving skills.

At CoachFrika, we aim to provide excellent coaching for teachers across Africa so they can be more intentional, efficient, and effective as teacher-leaders in their workplace. This, we believe, will lead to them deriving joy and happiness in their careers as it will accelerate their growth. Coachfrika also shared the view that the coaching will enhance the teachers' ability to make informed decisions about their career paths and sustainably raise problem-solving leaders in the future.

We provide coaching, we monitor and evaluate growth, and we get feedback from the stakeholders.

Mission
How:

❖ To recruit passionate educational/leadership coaches all over Africa region.

❖ To enrol teachers both in private and public spaces on our coaching platform.

❖ To offer hybrid coaching support for teachers across Africa.

❖ To monitor, report and share the progress of all enrolled teachers for visibility.
